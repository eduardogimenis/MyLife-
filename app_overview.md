# MyLife! App Overview

## Purpose
**MyLife!** is a personal timeline application designed to help users document and visualize their life's journey. It serves as a digital memory lane, allowing users to capture significant moments, categorize them, and see them laid out in a chronological timeline.

## Core Value Proposition
- **Visualize Your History:** See your life events in a continuous, reverse-chronological timeline.
- **Organize Memories:** Categorize events (Work, Education, Travel, etc.) for easy filtering and visual distinction.
- **Connect People:** Tag people in events to remember who was part of your journey.
- **Secure & Private:** All data is stored locally on the device using SwiftData, ensuring privacy.

## Target Audience
- Individuals who want to keep a record of their personal history.
- Users who appreciate a visual representation of their life's milestones.
- Privacy-conscious users who prefer local data storage.

## Key Capabilities
1.  **Event Tracking:** Create events with titles, dates (exact or approximate), notes, and locations.
2.  **Visual Timeline:** A custom-built timeline view that connects events visually.
3.  **Media Integration:** Attach photos to events to make memories vivid.
4.  **Data Management:** Import and export data (JSON support) to ensure data portability.
5.  **Customization:** Manage categories and people to tailor the app to your specific life structure.
